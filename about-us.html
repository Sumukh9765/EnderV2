About Us

EnderNodes is a game and dedicated server host that makes hosting easy, fast and affordable. We specialise in providing ultra high performance game servers for an ever expanding list of games.

All of our servers come with blazing fast SSD's and 1000Mbps broadband as standard which allows your server to boot and update at industry leading speeds.

Customer service and quality hardware are at the top of our priorities and thus we make sure all dedicated servers run high-end modern hardware and we take extreme pride in our support teams skill set.
